RGAMone

    RGAMone[ghtt_,ghbb_,ghWW_,gCH_,mCH_,x_,xmin_,xmax_,xlabel_][[i]]
This command evaluates

when there is dependence only on one parameter. The arguments ghtt, ghbb, ghWW and gCH are the h-top top, h-bottom bottom, h-WW and
couplings. Here, h represents to SM-like Higgs boson. Labels mCH and x indicate the charged scalar mass and the parameter to constraint, while xmin and xmax are the initial and final values defined by users and xlabel is used for indicates the axis-X label. Finally, [[i]] stands for confidence level, i=1 (2) indicates 1σ (2σ)
